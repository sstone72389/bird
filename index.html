Welcome to Origami 101!

A lesson prepared by Spencer Stone

Step 1: Supplies

Square paper and a willingness to learn

Step 2: Getting Started

Start with a square piece of origami paper. Align it diagonally as shown above.

Step 3: Fold Paper In Half

Fold the paper in half by folding the top corner to the bottom corner. You will now have an upside down triangle.

Step 4: Bring The Top Down

Fold the top part down so that the edge is about at the halfway mark. This part of our paper will become the wings.

Step 5: Turn it Over

Turn your paper over as such

Step 6: Fold Top Layer Up

Take the bottom corner of the top-most layer and fold it up as illustrated.

Step 7: Left to Right

Fold the your unfinished bird in half by folding the left side over to the right.
